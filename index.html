Research

What I work on

💥

Gamma-Ray Bursts

Detecting and characterizing GRBs using NASA's Swift Observatory, probing the most luminous explosions in the known universe.

🔭

High-Energy Neutrinos

Identifying astrophysical neutrino sources and linking IceCube detections to AGN jet activity and other extreme environments.

🌌

Active Galactic Nuclei

Studying AGN as potential cosmic ray accelerators, using multi-messenger observations to constrain emission models.

Selected work

2024

Swift/BAT GRB Detection Pipeline

Automated detection and rapid localization pipeline for gamma-ray burst events using the Burst Alert Telescope aboard Swift, enabling faster follow-up observations.

Swift-BAT Python GRBs
2023

AGN–Neutrino Correlation Study

Statistical cross-correlation of IceCube high-energy neutrino events with AGN catalogs to identify candidate sources and constrain hadronic emission models.

IceCube AGN Multi-messenger
2022

GRB Afterglow Spectral Modeling

Broadband spectral fitting of GRB afterglow emission from X-ray through optical, constraining circumburst density profiles and jet structure.
